Develop a deep learning weather model for individual weather stations across the United States, especially in regions of high elevation and high yearly snowfall totals.

The final model will be trained from historic snowfall and general weather data accessed from the NOAA through their public and free API key system. Model training was optimized and the resulting model was saved for future use.

Results of the final model are used to make real time snowfall predictions for a localized region around the weather station trained for. This will be directly streamed to a weather tracking application and or website for ease of use and effectiveness in results.
